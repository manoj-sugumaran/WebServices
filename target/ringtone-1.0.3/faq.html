What is ONMO Tone?

ONMO Tones puts you in control by giving you unlimited access to a world of sound. This is the new personalization service that allows you to set an exact part of a song as ringtone & notification. Choose from a large catalog of music and free sounds.

ENJOY:

- MUSIC: Select your favorite music and be creative. There’s no limit to the song you can choose. Tracks range from the biggest artists to very niche local musicians to soundtracks from your favorite TV shows and movies

- TODAY: Discover the stories behind every great music. Get to know interesting music facts about your favorite artists, songs, events, performances, this actual day in music and much more. You will know now also stories around the music that made the day!

- FREE TUNES: You can always use our free tunes, even if you’re not ready to subscribe.

- CUT: You know exactly which part of a song is most relevant to you. Create your own unique cut to impress your callers.

How do I activate the service?

  1. Select a sound that you like.
  2. You just need to select the part of the song you want to set
  3. Did you like this sound? Just click on set as ringtone or as notification
  4. Since you are a new subscriber, a confirmation popup will appear asking to activate the service, click on the subscribe
  5. Now you can continue and set a ringtone for specific contact.

Will I lose my settings if I unsubscribe and subscribe again later?

If you unsubscribe from the service, all the settings will be lost but we keep your library in case you subscribe again.

The service cannot be suspended temporarily.

Yes, you will get 3 days of grace if the charging fails on the renewal date.

To unsubscribe you need to go directly to Google Play and to the subscription management tab.

What is add to library?

Adding a tone to library helps you to find back your favorite tone and so you will be able to set them.

What is the difference between notification and ringtone?

The notification tone is a short 3 seconds tone that is mainly played for messages or basic notification. A ringtone is a longer tone, usually 20 seconds, that is played for incoming call. This is when you select a preview in ringtone it is longer than the preview of notification.
